The Next Heartstone Expansion “Across the Timeways”

The Hearthstone next expansion has officially been revealed, and it’s called Hearthstone Across the Timeways. This exciting update brings together iconic Warcraft heroes from both the past and future, uniting them in a timeline-bending adventure full of new cards, mechanics, and surprises. Players will once again join Chromie as she navigates rifts in time, pulling legendary characters from different eras straight into the Tavern. With a confirmed Hearthstone next expansion release date set for this season, fans can prepare for one of the most ambitious sets yet. The Hearthstone Across the Timeways expansion promises to shake up the meta and deliver unique gameplay possibilities that celebrate the history and future of the Warcraft universe.

Hearthstone Next Expansion Release Date

The highly anticipated Hearthstone Across the Timeways expansion has officially been confirmed to launch on November 4, 2025. Blizzard revealed the release date alongside a cinematic trailer that showcases Chromie journeying through multiple timelines to gather legendary Warcraft heroes. This expansion promises not only a unique time-bending storyline but also the introduction of 145 brand-new cards and fresh mechanics like Fabled and Rewind, adding new layers of strategy to the game. Mark your calendars, because the Hearthstone next expansion release date sets the stage for one of the most ambitious updates yet.

Hearthstone Across the Timeways Expansion

The Hearthstone Across the Timeways expansion takes players on a time-warping adventure where past, present, and future collide. This set introduces 145 new cards and debuts two groundbreaking keywords – Fabled and Rewind – that transform gameplay with fresh mechanics rooted in the theme of temporal manipulation.

New Keyword: Fabled

Fabled cards represent legendary Warcraft figures drawn from alternate timelines. Each class receives a Fabled Legendary hero such as Ranger General Sylvanas, Ranger Captain Alleria, and Ranger Initiate Vereesa, who come with extra synergy cards that automatically slot into your deck. Other highlights include Gelbin of Tomorrow, piloting a futuristic mech and summoning powerful auras with cards like Chronological Aura and Manifested Timeways.

New Keyword: Rewind

Rewind introduces time travel into Hearthstone. This keyword allows players to replay cards for a different outcome, whether it’s fixing a misfired spell or giving a minion a second chance to shine. The mechanic captures the essence of rewriting history, letting you decide whether to keep the result or bend fate by rewinding the timeline.

Bend Time to Your Will

Beyond the new keywords, the expansion adds unique effects that embrace the time-warp theme. Timeway Warden can imprison a minion for 10,000 turns, while Divergence splits a card into two alternate versions, creating strategic depth and unpredictable scenarios. These mechanics push players to experiment with new ways of controlling the battlefield.

Allies of the Timeways

The narrative also shines with the return of Timelord Nozdormu, representing the Bronze Dragonflight. His battle against Murozond, his corrupted future self, captures the duality of time. Supporting cards like Soldier of the Bronze and Soldier of the Infinite reflect the ongoing clash of timelines, reinforcing the expansion’s lore-driven theme.

Altogether, the Hearthstone Across the Timeways expansion blends new mechanics, iconic heroes, and a gripping storyline into one of the most innovative updates yet. By combining nostalgia with bold mechanics like Fabled and Rewind, this expansion gives both veteran and new players countless ways to rewrite Hearthstone history.

Conclusion

The Hearthstone Across the Timeways expansion brings one of the most ambitious updates in the game’s history. With the addition of 145 new cards, the introduction of Fabled and Rewind mechanics, and iconic Warcraft heroes returning from alternate timelines, the expansion offers a perfect mix of nostalgia and innovation. Players can bend time to their will, experiment with creative strategies, and immerse themselves in a story where past, present, and future collide. Whether you’re a veteran of the Tavern or a newcomer, this expansion guarantees a fresh and exciting way to play Hearthstone.
