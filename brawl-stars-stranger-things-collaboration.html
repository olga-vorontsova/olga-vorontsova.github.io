Brawl Stars Stranger Things Collaboration

The Brawl Stars Stranger Things Crossover brings one of gaming's most anticipated collaborations to the Star Park universe, fundamentally transforming the game’s aesthetic and introducing unique new challenges. Season 44 kicks off this massive event, merging the chaotic energy of the Brawlers with the eerie, retro atmosphere of Hawkins, Indiana. This event is not just cosmetic; it introduces a limited-time game mode and collectible rewards that players must efficiently farm.

This comprehensive guide provides an overview of everything you need to know about the collaboration. We will analyze the impact of the New Brawl Stars Season 44 on the current meta, detail how to acquire the exclusive Stranger Things Brawl Stars Skins, and review the performance of the New Brawlers in Brawl Stars. Based on community response, these limited-time additions will be highly sought after and may never return, demanding immediate attention from every dedicated player.

Whether you are aiming to complete the exclusive quests or simply want to optimize your brawlers with the most powerful new skins, this guide ensures you are fully prepared to dominate the Upside Down and maximize your seasonal rewards.

Event Duration and Vecna's Influence

The Brawl Stars Stranger Things Crossover is a limited-time event, running for just under a month. The collaboration starts on November 6th and will conclude on December 3rd. During this period, Vecna's influence has breached the Brawl Stars universe, tearing open a portal to the Upside Down.

Players should maximize their activity within this timeframe to discover a whole host of themed modes, maps, boss battles, skins, power-ups, and rewards. Given the exclusive nature of these rewards, prioritizing event quests is highly recommended to secure all items before the portal closes permanently.

New Event Content – Upside Showdown Mode

The core gameplay experience introduced by the Brawl Stars Stranger Things Crossover is the brand-new, limited-time Upside Showdown Mode. This exciting mode puts a fresh twist on the traditional Showdown experience by cleverly combining familiar survival gameplay with roguelite progression mechanics. This integration makes every match of Upside Showdown Mode feel unique and strategically challenging.

A single match consists of five increasingly difficult rounds. The roguelite element comes into play before entering a new stage: players are given the strategic choice to select a temporary Power Card to enhance their Brawler for the upcoming round. These cards offer a range of temporary buffs, from stronger attacks and defensive barriers to improved mobility and utility effects.

Progression is highly competitive: only the top four players in each stage are allowed to advance, while the others face immediate elimination. The ultimate goal is to survive through all five rounds to secure the best rewards and make it back to the "right side up". If you successfully emerge as the ultimate victor, you will be greeted with the iconic message: "Winner, winner, waffle dinner!", celebrating your dominance in the New Brawl Stars Season 44.

Hawkins Hunt: New PVE Boss Fights

As part of the Brawl Stars Stranger Things Crossover, Vecna's influence has breached the universe, unleashing new threats. The central PVE event for the New Brawl Stars Season 44 is Hawkins Hunt, a mode where players must team up with two other Brawlers (forming a 3-player squad) to overcome the horrors leaking from the Upside Down.

The event is structured into five stages of increasing difficulty. Teamwork is essential, as "friends don't lie," and players must collaborate to succeed. Successfully winning each stage rewards players with "sweet Waffles", which serve as the special event currency for this Brawl Stars Stranger Things event.

During these Boss Fights, players will face three major antagonists from the collaboration:

  • The Demogorgon: One of the most iconic monsters from the Stranger Things series.
  • Vecna: The main antagonist himself will also emerge as a powerful enemy during the battles.
  • Spirit Frank: A special version of the fan-favorite Epic Brawler, described as Frank being "possessed by a Spirit Knight and rampaging across the Arena".

Waffles: The Upside Down Event Currency

The Brawl Stars Stranger Things Crossover introduces a unique, limited-time event currency: Waffles. Once a "noble breakfast food", this currency now functions similarly to previous food-themed items like Pizza Slices and Sushi Rolls. To help everyone get started, players will receive some free Waffles at the beginning of the event.

How to Earn Waffles

Throughout the New Brawl Stars Season 44 event, players can earn Waffles in multiple ways:

  • Participating in the new Upside Showdown mode.
  • Successfully completing Hawkins Hunt Boss Fights against Vecna, the Demogorgon, and Spirit Frank.
  • Finishing Mega Quests and achieving Daily Wins.
  • Claiming free Waffles from the Shop.

How to Spend Waffles

Your hard-earned Waffles have several key uses in the event shop. The primary use is to purchase Brawler Decks. These decks are essential as they unlock Brawlers for you to use in the event-specific modes, Upside Showdown and Hawkins Hunt. Waffles can also be exchanged for Card Packs and specific Ability Cards to upgrade your Brawlers. Additionally, the currency can be spent to unlock various power-ups, cosmetics, and collectibles related to the event.

Themed Maps: Welcome to the Upside Down

To fully immerse players in the Brawl Stars Stranger Things Crossover, the event introduces specially themed maps for both standard 3v3 and Showdown game modes. These maps are not entirely new locations but rather transformations of familiar areas within Starr Park.

The New Brawl Stars Season 44 event adds a darker, Stranger Things-inspired aesthetic to the gameplay. Players can expect eerie visuals, darker atmospheres, and new strategic elements directly inspired by the Upside Down. This thematic overhaul ensures that every match, even in standard modes, feels fresh, immersive, and aligned with the spooky tone of the collaboration.

FAQ

The event features two main modes: Upside Showdown, a survival mode with roguelite Power Card progression, and Hawkins Hunt, a 3-player PVE mode where you fight bosses like Vecna, the Demogorgon, and Spirit Frank.

The main Eleven Lumi Skin is the final reward (Tier 50) on the paid Brawl Pass. The Retro Eleven Lumi (Tier 46) and Thrilled Eleven Lumi (Tier 48) skins are exclusive to the Brawl Pass Plus track.

Waffles are the special event currency. They are used in the shop to buy Brawler Decks (which unlock Brawlers for the event modes), Card Packs, cosmetics, and other collectibles.

The event runs from November 6th until December 3rd. All exclusive content, including skins and modes, will likely be unavailable after this date.
